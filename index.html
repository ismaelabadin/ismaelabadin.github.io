Feel free to check out some of my projects below!

I have been making websites since I was in middle school, when I used to participate in National History Day. Back then, I would use website builders such as Weebly to design and perfect my projects. During my undergrad I pursued a different path, but since graduating I've come full-circle and have delved straight into becoming a React developer.
