Pokedex Project

Tech Stack:

In this project, I designed a "Pokégame", where 4 "Pokécards" with different Pokémon are dealt at random to two battling players. This was one of the first React apps I built, and was a fun little side project for me to practice my React and CSS skills. The winner of the battle was simply determined by summing up the experience points of all their Pokémon.
